What is fusion?

Fusion is the reaction which powers the sun and all other stars

And now we’re working to harness it on Earth, to create a world powered by clean, limitless energy for all.

How does it work?

Fusion is a potential form of power generation that uses the energy released by forcing together lightweight atoms until they fuse together into heavier atoms. It’s the same reaction that powers stars.

Fusion requires extreme temperatures and pressure to occur but it produces more energy per gram of fuel than any other fuel source on the planet.

When will it be possible?

Fusion won’t be immediate

There are still challenges to overcome – but for the first time practical fusion is within our reach. Addressing these challenges has led to innovative new approaches to fusion.

Fusion powerplants will work with renewables to give us the energy the world needs. Clean, safe and abundant energy, anywhere in the world. Fusion energy will help us fight climate change while ensuring our way of life.
