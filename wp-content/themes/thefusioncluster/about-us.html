What is The Fusion Cluster?

Sparking the fusion revolution. Together.

Renewable energy is great, but the challenge of climate change is so vast, it’s not enough. We need fusion. The planet, society, the future needs fusion. And at The Fusion Cluster we’re helping to create it.

Why does it exist?

Our vision is a world powered by sustainable energy

Low carbon, self-sustaining, fusion energy will provide a baseload of future-proof power. It will work with renewables to give us the energy the world needs. It will help us fight climate change while ensuring our way of life. And the UK will be a leader making it happen – from theory & science to technology & reality
